# Report — Prompt A, run 2

Based on the couple's priorities: budget under $8,000 for venue rental, guest count up to 120 people, vibe romantic but not too corporate, must have outdoor ceremony option, catering should be in-house or approved vendors list, and availability of outdoor spaces, I recommend venues 3 (Lakeview Pavilion), 4 (Thornfield Manor), and 9 (The Belvedere).

**Venue 3 (Lakeview Pavilion):** A lakeside pavilion with a capacity of up to 100 guests that offers an intimate setting, is very affordable ($2,500 for a weekend or $3,600 with outdoor ceremony), and features stunning views. The price starts at $2,500 (Friday–Sunday) and has no indoor backup, so it's perfect for the couple's budget-conscious needs.

**Venue 4 (Thornfield Manor):** This historic manor house offers an exclusive setting with a capacity of up to 170 guests and includes in-house catering only. It fits the romantic vibe the couple is looking for, has no outdoor backup, but requires guests to bring their own vendors or hire a contracted team. Pricing starts at $18,000 (ballroom rental) with catering packages extra.

**Venue 9 (The Belvedere):** This waterfront venue offers stunning views and an indoor/outdoor setup, has a capacity of up to 220 guests, and is known for being very elegant. However, it's more expensive than the couple's budget (pricing TBD) and requires in-house catering only.
