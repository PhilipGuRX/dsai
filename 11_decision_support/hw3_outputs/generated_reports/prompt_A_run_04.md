Based on the couple's priorities and venue descriptions, I recommend three venues:

2. **The Foundry at Millworks**: A trendy industrial-chic venue that fits 250 capacity with bring-your-own vendors and a rooftop available for cocktail hour (though no formal parking lot). This venue is very affordable but might not offer the same level of elegance as some other options.

3. **The Glasshouse Conservatory**: A dramatic all-glass event space surrounded by botanical gardens, offering 140 capacity with outdoor garden area and catering open. The venue has a minimalist feel with lots of parking on site. This option is more modern and trendy but might not offer the same level of traditional elegance as some other venues.

All three venues fit within the budget of under $8,000 for a wedding. However, it's essential to discuss catering options and specific needs (e.g., outdoor ceremony backup) with each venue to ensure they align with the couple's expectations.
