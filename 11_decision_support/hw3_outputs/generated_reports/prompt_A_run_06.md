# Report — Prompt A, run 6

Considering the couple's priorities and given their budget of $8,000 for venue rental, I would recommend the following venues:

1. **The Ivy House**: A garden venue with a capacity of 100 guests, offering an intimate setting that fits within their budget. The venue also has easy street parking and can be reached by shuttle from nearby lots if necessary. While it's not as formal or luxurious as some other options, the Ivy House provides a unique and charming atmosphere at a reasonable cost.

2. **The Glasshouse Conservatory**: An all-glass event space with a capacity of 140 guests, offering dramatic views and flexibility in terms of outdoor ceremonies. The venue has ample parking on site and is known for its high-quality catering. While it may require more planning due to the open layout, The Glasshouse Conservatory provides an unforgettable experience at a budget-friendly price point.

3. **The Foundry at Millworks**: A trendy industrial-chic converted factory venue that fits 250 guests with plenty of parking options (street and nearby garage). While it may not offer traditional wedding amenities like a formal wedding ceremony area, The Foundry is an excellent choice for those seeking a unique and modern atmosphere. With its affordable rental fee ($5,000) and flexible catering options, this venue offers great value for the couple's budget.
